Beppu

Steaming Springs Meet Tranquility

Things to Do in Beppu

DAY 2

Yufuin

After a rejuvenating stay in Beppu, immerse yourself in the charming atmosphere of Yufuin. This picturesque town offers a delightful escape with its iconic Yunotsubo Street, perfect for shopping and dining. Capture the beauty of Kinrin Lake and explore the countless photo opportunities Yufuin has to offer.

DAY 3

Beppu Ropeway

Unwind in Beppu: From Sky-High Views to Serene Hot Springs Experience the thrill of ascending 1,375 meters aboard the Beppu Ropeway, where panoramic vistas await. Afterward, rejuvenate your senses at Ichinoide Kaikan, a renowned hot spring facility offering unparalleled relaxation. This perfect blend of adventure and tranquility will leave you feeling refreshed and renewed.

DAY 4

Chinoike Jigoku & Beach Sand Bath

On your second day, discover the unique allure of Beppu. Begin with a visit to Chinoike Jigoku, a captivating volcanic landscape renowned for its vibrant hot spring waters and unique culinary experiences. Afterward, unwind with a rejuvenating black sand bath on Beppu's pristine beach, where warm grains gently cradle your body while offering stunning coastal views.

DAY 5

Fukuoka

Before your journey ends, OUS invites you to savor the finest Tonkatsu Fukuoka has to offer. A delightful culinary treat awaits as a token of our appreciation.

Nagasaki

A Symphony of Water and History

Nagasaki Highlights

DAY 2

Kashima

Your journey begins at the revered Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine, offering a spiritual experience akin to the renowned Yutoku Inari Shrine.

DAY 3

Nagasaki Atomic Bomb Museum

Commence your day with a poignant visit to the Nagasaki Atomic Bomb Museum, a solemn reflection on history and the enduring spirit of the city. Immerse yourself in the stories of resilience and understand the profound significance of water in Nagasaki's journey. Conclude the day with a delightful culinary experience, savoring the renowned Nagasaki champon noodles at the iconic Nagasakijunten restaurant.

DAY 4

Mt. Nabekanmuri Park

Your second day in Nagasaki promises an unforgettable experience. Discover the enchanting Megane Bridge and savor the city's renowned buns in Shinchi Chinatown. Immerse yourself in history at the Former Hong Kong & Shanghai Bank Nagasaki Branch Museum and Oura Cathedral, where you can also indulge in the finest castella. Crown your day with breathtaking vistas from Mt. Nabekanmuri Park.

DAY 5

Fukuoka

Savor a farewell feast of fresh seafood, carefully curated by OUS. Enjoy a complimentary culinary experience before your departure, with the freedom to explore additional dining options.
